AI-Powered Chatbot

A Project Proposal Overview

The Challenge & The Solution

Organizations often struggle with providing instant, accurate responses when information is scattered across documents. This project proposes a unified, intelligent chatbot to automate interactions and streamline information retrieval, combining multiple modern technologies into one seamless solution.
